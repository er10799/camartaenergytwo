Not every property is ideal for solar. We'll honestly tell you if your home or business is a good candidate—and if not, we'll explain why and what alternatives exist.

What's Included in Your Free Consultation

Our comprehensive consultation process ensures you have all the information needed to make a confident decision.

Complete Property Assessment

We analyze your roof orientation, pitch, available space, shading patterns throughout the day and seasons, structural considerations, and electrical service capacity. This determines your property's true solar potential and optimal system size.

Detailed Energy Usage Analysis

We review your electricity bills from the past 12 months to understand your consumption patterns, seasonal variations, and rate structure. This ensures we design a system matched to your actual needs—not a generic estimate.

Accurate Savings Projection

Get a detailed breakdown showing expected annual energy production, monthly and annual savings, payback period, 25-year savings total, and return on investment. All projections based on real data, not generic estimates.

Incentives & Financing Options

We explain all available federal, provincial, and municipal incentive programs you qualify for, plus financing options including loans, leases, and power purchase agreements. Our goal is making solar affordable for your specific budget.

Custom System Design

Receive a preliminary system design showing panel placement, equipment specifications, and expected performance. We optimize for aesthetics, production, and budget—not just slapping panels on your roof.

Timeline & Next Steps

Clear explanation of the entire process from permitting to installation to activation. You'll know exactly what to expect, how long it takes, and what your involvement entails at each stage.

How the Consultation Process Works

Simple, transparent, and designed to fit your schedule.

1

Initial Contact

Call us or fill out the consultation request form. We'll schedule a brief 15-20 minute phone or video call to discuss your goals and answer basic questions.

2

On-Site Visit

We visit your property to assess your roof, take measurements, evaluate shading, and inspect your electrical panel. Most site visits take 30-45 minutes.

3

Detailed Proposal

Within 48 hours, you receive a comprehensive proposal including system design, equipment specifications, pricing, savings analysis, and financing options.

4

Review & Decide

Follow-up call to review the proposal and answer any questions. Take all the time you need to decide— absolutely no pressure. The decision is 100% yours.
