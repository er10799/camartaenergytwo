Why Schedule a Consultation?

Going solar is a significant decision, and you deserve accurate, honest information. Our free consultation service provides a comprehensive analysis of your property's solar potential without any pressure or obligation.

Whether you're just exploring the idea or ready to move forward, we'll provide the insights you need to make an informed decision about solar energy for your home or business.

What's Included in Your Free Consultation

Property Assessment

We analyze your roof orientation, shading, available space, and structural considerations to determine your property's solar potential and optimal system configuration.

Energy Usage Analysis

We review your electricity bills and consumption patterns to design a system that matches your actual energy needs and maximizes your savings potential.

Savings Projection

Get a detailed breakdown of your expected energy production, cost savings, payback period, and long-term financial benefits. We show you exactly what solar means for your bottom line.

Financing & Incentives

We explain all available financing options, government rebates, tax credits, and incentive programs. Our goal is to make solar affordable and accessible for your budget.

What to Expect During Your Consultation

Step 1: Initial phone or video call to discuss your goals and answer basic questions (15-20 minutes)

Step 2: On-site visit to assess your property and take measurements (30-45 minutes)

Step 3: Detailed proposal delivered within 48 hours, including system design, pricing, and savings analysis

Step 4: Follow-up discussion to review proposal and answer any questions—with zero pressure
