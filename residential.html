Why Go Solar at Home?

Homeowners across Western Canada are discovering the freedom and savings that come with residential solar. By generating your own clean electricity, you can reduce or even eliminate your monthly power bills, increase your property value, and protect yourself from rising utility rates.

  • Reduce energy bills by up to 80% - Start seeing savings immediately
  • Increase home value - Solar homes sell faster and for more
  • Energy independence - Generate your own power, reduce grid reliance
  • Environmental impact - Reduce your carbon footprint significantly
  • Government incentives - Take advantage of rebates and tax credits
