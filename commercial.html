Why Commercial Solar Makes Business Sense

Commercial properties are ideal for solar installations. Large roof spaces, high daytime energy consumption, and favorable incentive structures create a perfect storm for exceptional return on investment.

Unlike residential installations, commercial solar systems benefit from economies of scale, accelerated depreciation, and commercial-specific incentive programs that dramatically improve financial returns.

Average Commercial Results:
  • 50-70% reduction in electricity costs
  • 5-7 year payback period
  • 25+ years of clean energy production
  • 15-20% increase in property value

Financial Benefits That Drive Bottom-Line Results

Commercial solar isn't just an environmental decision—it's a smart financial investment that delivers measurable returns from day one.

Massive Cost Savings

Commercial electricity rates in Alberta average $0.12-0.18/kWh and rising annually. Solar locks in your energy costs at $0.03-0.05/kWh for 25+ years. For a business using 50,000 kWh/year, that's $50,000-75,000 in savings over the system's lifetime.

Tax Advantages

Take advantage of accelerated Capital Cost Allowance (CCA) at 50% declining balance, allowing you to depreciate your solar investment faster. Combined with federal and provincial incentives, effective system cost can be reduced by 40-50%.

Property Value Increase

Commercial properties with solar installations command 10-20% higher valuations and attract quality tenants willing to pay premium rates. Solar is a capital improvement that increases asset value while reducing operating expenses—a double win for property owners.

Energy Price Protection

Electricity prices are volatile and trend upward over time. Solar provides price certainty, protecting your business from rate increases and allowing for accurate long-term budgeting. Lock in predictable energy costs for decades.

Competitive Advantage

Demonstrate environmental leadership and differentiate your brand. 73% of consumers prefer to do business with environmentally responsible companies. Solar strengthens your market position and appeals to eco-conscious customers and partners.

Positive Cash Flow

With financing options and incentives, many commercial solar systems are cash-flow positive from month one. Your energy savings exceed your loan payment, putting money back into your business immediately rather than waiting for payback.

Commercial Solar Incentives in Alberta

Alberta offers multiple incentive programs designed specifically for commercial solar installations. Our team navigates these programs to maximize your financial return.

Federal Commercial Programs

Accelerated Capital Cost Allowance (CCA)

Class 43.1 and 43.2 assets allow for 50% declining balance depreciation on eligible solar equipment, significantly reducing tax burden in early years.

Clean Energy Investment Tax Credit

Up to 30% refundable tax credit on eligible renewable energy equipment including solar panels, inverters, and installation costs.

Canada Infrastructure Bank Financing

Low-interest financing options for large commercial solar projects, making upfront costs more manageable.

Alberta Provincial Programs

Municipal & Business Grant Programs

Edmonton and Calgary offer municipal grants and property tax incentives for commercial solar installations. Programs vary by municipality but can provide $10,000-50,000 in additional funding.

Net Metering for Commercial

Commercial net metering allows you to sell excess solar generation back to the grid at retail rates, further improving ROI and enabling larger system sizes than your consumption alone would justify.

Emissions Performance Credit Program

Large industrial facilities can generate carbon offset credits through solar installations, creating an additional revenue stream or compliance mechanism.

Our team handles all incentive applications and paperwork—maximizing your benefits with zero administrative burden.
