Solar System Maintenance & Support

Keep your solar investment performing at peak efficiency with professional maintenance

Protect Your Investment

Solar panels are incredibly durable and low-maintenance, but like any valuable asset, they perform best with proper care. Our comprehensive maintenance programs ensure your system operates at maximum efficiency for its entire 25+ year lifespan.

Whether you installed your system with us or another provider, our certified technicians can help you maintain peak performance, identify potential issues early, and maximize your energy production and savings.

Maintenance Benefits

  • Maintain optimal energy production
  • Extend system lifespan
  • Early detection of issues
  • Preserve warranty coverage
  • Maximize return on investment
  • Priority service response

Our Maintenance Services

Annual Inspections

Comprehensive yearly checkups including panel cleaning, electrical testing, inverter diagnostics, and performance analysis. We identify and address issues before they impact your production.

Performance Monitoring

24/7 remote monitoring to track your system's energy production. We alert you to any performance issues and can often diagnose problems remotely, saving you time and money.

Repairs & Upgrades

Fast, reliable repair service for any issue—from inverter replacement to panel repairs. We also offer system upgrades including battery storage integration and capacity expansion.

Protect your solar investment with professional care. Our maintenance plans include annual inspections, cleaning, priority service, and discounted repairs.
