Why Solar Maintenance Matters

While solar panels are incredibly durable, they're not maintenance-free. Environmental factors like dust, snow, bird droppings, and extreme weather can reduce efficiency by 15-25% if left unaddressed. Regular professional maintenance keeps your system operating at maximum output.

More importantly, most manufacturer warranties require documented annual maintenance. Skipping maintenance doesn't just reduce performance—it can void your warranty coverage entirely, leaving you exposed to expensive repair costs.

Did You Know?

Unmaintained solar systems lose an average of 0.5-1% of their efficiency per year due to soiling alone. Over 25 years, that's up to 25% lost production—equivalent to losing thousands of dollars in energy savings.

Comprehensive Maintenance Services

From routine inspections to emergency repairs, we provide complete solar system care for residential and commercial installations across Western Canada.

Annual Inspections

Comprehensive yearly checkups including visual inspections, electrical testing, inverter diagnostics, panel cleaning, mounting hardware checks, and performance analysis. We identify and address potential issues before they impact your energy production.

Professional Cleaning

Specialized panel cleaning using purified water and soft brushes that won't scratch or damage panels. We safely remove dirt, pollen, bird droppings, and environmental buildup that reduces efficiency. Most systems see 10-15% production increase after cleaning.

24/7 Performance Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of your system's energy production with instant alerts for performance issues. Our team tracks output, identifies underperformance, and can often diagnose problems remotely—saving you time and service call costs.

Repairs & Troubleshooting

Fast, reliable repair service for inverter issues, panel replacements, wiring problems, and electrical faults. Our Master Electricians diagnose and repair any system issue, regardless of original installer. Most repairs completed within 48 hours.

Winter Maintenance

Alberta-specific winter services including snow removal (when necessary), ice dam prevention, and cold-weather performance optimization. We ensure your system continues producing efficiently even during harsh Western Canadian winters.

System Upgrades

Expand your system capacity, add battery storage, upgrade inverters to newer technology, or integrate smart home energy management. We help you maximize your solar investment as your energy needs evolve and technology advances.
