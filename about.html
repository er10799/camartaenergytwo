About Camarta Energy

Your trusted partner for clean, renewable solar energy solutions across Western Canada

Our Story

Founded over two decades ago, Camarta Energy has grown from a small team of passionate electricians into Western Canada's leading solar installation company. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make clean, renewable energy accessible and affordable for every home and business.

Today, we've installed over 5,000 solar systems across Alberta, Saskatchewan, Manitoba, and British Columbia, helping families and businesses reduce their energy costs while contributing to a sustainable future. Our team of Master and Journeyman Electricians brings unmatched expertise, precision, and dedication to every project.

Our Commitment

At Camarta Energy, we believe that solar power should be simple, reliable, and built to last. That's why every installation is backed by our comprehensive 25-year warranty and supported by a team that's available whenever you need us.

We're not just installing solar panels—we're building relationships and powering communities. Our customer-first approach means transparent pricing, honest communication, and a commitment to excellence that has earned us a 98% customer satisfaction rating.
