Powering a Sustainable Tomorrow

At Camarta Energy, we believe the future belongs to clean, renewable energy. Every day, we work toward a world where homes and businesses across Western Canada are powered by the sun—reducing carbon emissions, lowering energy costs, and creating lasting environmental impact.

Our purpose is simple yet profound: to make solar energy accessible, affordable, and reliable for everyone. We're not just in the business of installing solar panels—we're in the business of transforming how communities think about and use energy.

Through innovation, expertise, and unwavering commitment to quality, we empower homeowners and businesses to take control of their energy future while contributing to a healthier planet for generations to come.

What We Envision

We envision a future where every building in Western Canada harnesses the power of the sun. A future where clean energy is the norm, not the exception. Where energy independence is within reach for all, and where our collective environmental footprint shrinks with each installation.

We see a tomorrow where rising utility costs are a thing of the past, where grid outages don't disrupt daily life, and where our children inherit a planet powered by renewable resources. This vision drives everything we do.

How We Deliver

Our mission is to deliver exceptional solar solutions through expert installation, honest service, and uncompromising quality. We achieve this by employing only certified Master and Journeyman Electricians, using premium equipment, and standing behind every installation with industry-leading warranties.

We educate our customers, simplify the solar journey, and ensure every system is designed for maximum performance and longevity. From consultation to installation to ongoing support, we're committed to exceeding expectations at every step.

Our Story

Camarta Energy's journey began over two decades ago in Edmonton, Alberta, with a small team of electricians who saw the untapped potential of solar energy in Western Canada. While others doubted solar's viability in our climate, we knew better.

Our founders started with a simple belief: that every home and business deserves access to clean, affordable, and reliable energy. Armed with electrical expertise and a passion for renewable energy, they installed their first residential solar system in 2003. That single installation sparked a movement.

Word spread quickly. Customers loved the savings, the environmental impact, and the energy independence that solar provided. From that first installation, we've grown to become Western Canada's most trusted solar company, with over 5,000 systems installed across four provinces.

What Sets Camarta Energy Apart

The competitive advantages that make us Western Canada's most trusted solar company

Master-Level Expertise

Unlike competitors who hire subcontractors, our installations are performed exclusively by certified Master and Journeyman Electricians. This isn't just a differentiator—it's a guarantee of quality, safety, and code compliance. Our team's electrical expertise ensures every system is installed correctly the first time.

Climate-Optimized Systems

We design every system specifically for Western Canada's unique climate. From extreme cold to heavy snow loads, we understand the challenges our region presents. Our installations use cold-weather optimized components and mounting systems engineered to withstand harsh conditions while maximizing energy production year-round.

Industry-Leading Warranties

Our 25-year comprehensive warranty isn't just industry-leading—it's backed by a financially stable, locally-based company that will be here when you need us. We stand behind our work because we believe in the quality of our installations. Most competitors offer shorter warranties or use third-party warranty providers. We're different.

True Turnkey Service

From initial consultation to final grid connection, we handle everything. Permits, inspections, utility paperwork, rebate applications—you don't lift a finger. Many competitors require customers to navigate bureaucracy themselves. We believe solar should be easy, so we manage every detail from start to finish.

Lifetime Customer Support

Our relationship doesn't end at installation. We provide ongoing system monitoring, annual maintenance, responsive customer service, and technical support for the life of your system. When you call, you reach our local team—not an offshore call center. You're not just a number; you're part of the Camarta Energy family.

Premium Equipment Only
